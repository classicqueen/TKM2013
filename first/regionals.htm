FRC Overview Rebound Rumble Build Season Regionals The Robot FIRST Overview

This year, the McKinley Robotics Team competed in two FIRST Robotics Competition regionals: Hawaii Regional and Central Valley Regional.

Choose a regional to view by clicking on the designated image.

 
Hawaii Regional
March 22-24, 2012
Honolulu, HI
Central Valley Regional
April 5-7, 2012
Madera, CA

Join us as we embark on our first FRC regional at our hometown; competing against 39 other teams from Hawaii, Louisiana, and Taiwan. A whole new competition awaits for our very own drive team as they prepare to rebound rumble!

Mahalo to teams 2090 and 3881, for being great alliance partners in the elimination matches, literally until the very end. Going through three sets of tiebreaker is tiring, but you folks gave your best efforts on each match that we applaud your tenacity.

Follow us in our adventure to California - before, during, and after the inaugural event of the Central Valley Regional. Even though Team 368 had prior experience from Hawaii Regional, it's no easy journey for the only Hawaii team with 47 teams competing; some of which are previous world champions.

Mahalo to teams 1266 and 2643, for being amazing alliance partners in our 8th seeded alliance for elimination matches. We knew we were going against the 1st seeded alliance in quarterfinals, but we made sure they worked hard for those wins by working together in stacking a lot of points through hybrid, tele-op, and balancing.
