FRC Overview Rebound Rumble Build Season Regionals The Robot FIRST Overview


Rebound Rumble is played on a 27' x 54' field by two competing alliances made up of three robots (teams) each. The match runs for 2 minutes and 15 seconds, with teams trying to score as many basketballs into four hoops as they can. The hoops sit at three different heights, and the higher up the hoop, the more points Alliances get.

This year, the introduction of the Microsoft Kinect has made the game more unique. The first 15 seconds of the match is a 'Hybrid' period, in which robots run independently of the drivers, but they can also be controlled using the Kinect. Points scored during this Hybrid period are worth extra. Once this period is over, drivers can start controlling their robots and continue to score points.

Three bridges, one for each alliance and a Coopertition bridge, are placed along the center of the field. In the last moments of a Qualification match, two robots from each Alliance will try to balance on their own bridge for extra points, while a third robot can balance on the Coopertition bridge with a robot from another Alliance to gain additional ranking points.
